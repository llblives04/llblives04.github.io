    Our Proposal

    Proposal

    We would like to see more daycares in high schools. Many teens who get pregnant tend to dropout of school because they may have to care for their children on their own and not have the necessary tools to do so. Implementing our idea would reduce the droput rate of pregnant teens and ultimately increase the amount of women in the workforce. A daycare in highschool also serves as an educational class, by giving other students exposure to parenthood. We want to show how everyone can make a diffrence and those who are single parents in high school or college should have the help they need.
